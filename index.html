Conway's Game of Life





Directions:

Click on any cell to make it live. Press the 'Start' button. Cells that are alive will be colored. At any point, you may press the 'Pause' button. To start a new game press the 'New Game' button. At any point during the game you may click on a cell to make it live.

The Rules:

Any live cell with less than two live neighbours dies. Mimicks underpopulation.

Any live cell with two or three live neighbours lives to the next generation.

Any live cell with more than three live neighbours dies. Mimicks overcrowding.

Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbours becomes alive. Mimicks birth.
